1-1l2 cups graham cracker crumbs
3 Tbsp. sugar
1l3 cup butter, melted
4 pkg. f8 02. eachi PHILADELPHIA Cream
Cheese, softened
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
4 eggs
Heat oven to 325F. UGOCI
Combine graham crumbs, 3 Tbsp. sugar and
butterr press onto bottom of 9-inch springform pan.
Beat cream cheese, 1 cup sugar and vanilla with
mixer until blended. Add eggs, 1 at a time, mixing
on low speed after each just until blended. Pour
over crust.
Bake 55 min. or until center is almost set. Run knife
around rim of pan to loosen cake: cool before
removing rim. Refrigerate cheesecake 4 hours.
